About LUXEMBOURG EMBASSY

Embassy of Luxembourg in Ethiopia

Location and Contact Information


The Embassy of Luxembourg in Ethiopia is situated in the heart of Addis Ababa on Cape Verde Street, P.O. Box 12813. This office serves as the only Luxembourg diplomatic representation in Ethiopia and handles all bilateral and consular matters for Luxembourg citizens and Ethiopian residents alike.
The Embassy of Luxembourg in Addis Ababa offers a full range of services, including:

- Visa processing for Ethiopian nationals traveling to Luxembourg
- Issuance and renewal of Luxembourg passports for citizens abroad
- Notarial acts and legalization of official documents
- Registration of Luxembourg residents living in Ethiopia
- Emergency assistance for Luxembourg citizens in distress

Please contact the embassy directly to confirm service availability and required documentation.
